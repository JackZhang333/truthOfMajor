# Design Context

## Users
- **Primary**: High school students preparing for Chinese college entrance exams (高考)
- **Context**: Students under stress, seeking reliable information about college majors
- **Goal**: Help them make informed decisions about their academic and career paths

## Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Warm, approachable, trustworthy like a knowledgeable senior student
- **Tone**: Encouraging, informative, without being condescending
- **3-word summary**: Young, lively, trustworthy (年轻、活泼、可信)
- **Emotional goals**: Reduce anxiety about major selection, build confidence in decisions

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Young and lively (更年轻活泼) - not the default corporate shadcn look
- **Current state**: Default shadcn/ui styling with card grids and muted colors
- **Anti-reference**: Avoid looking like AI-generated template (no generic card grids, no hero metrics with gradient text)
- **Theme**: Light mode primary (most education platforms in China are light)

## Design Principles
1. **Approachable over Authoritative**: Feel like peer advice, not institutional decree
2. **Clarity over Decoration**: Information hierarchy that helps stressed students find answers quickly
3. **Youthful but Respectful**: Lively design that doesn't trivialize an important life decision
4. **Content-First**: Design serves the expert advice and real experiences, not the other way around
